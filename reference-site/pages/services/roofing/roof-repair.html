Why timely roof repair matters

Even small roof issues can quickly lead to costly damage. Timely repairs protect your home and prevent minor problems from turning into major expenses.

Signs of roof damage you shouldn’t ignore:

  • Rotten roof decking
    ‍    Wood weakened by trapped water.
  • Mold growth in attic insulation
    ‍    Moisture creates unhealthy conditions.
  • Damaged drywall and ceilings
    ‍    Interior walls stained or collapsing.
  • Structural compromise
    ‍    Serious weakening of your home’s frame.

‍

Madison’s freeze-thaw cycles make things worse—water enters, freezes, expands, and splits materials further. Whether it's wind, hail, or just wear and tear, early repair is the key to protecting your home.

Common roof repairs we handle

From leaks to storm damage, we repair all types of issues on shingle and metal roofs. Our expert team quickly diagnoses the problem and restores your roof’s integrity.

Leaky flashing
Around chimneys and vents causing water intrusion.

Storm or wind-damaged shingles
Shingles lifted, cracked, or missing after severe weather.

Sagging roof decks
Soft spots and structural weakness from trapped moisture.

Hail damage
Bruised shingles, dents, and granule loss.

Skylight and vent boot leaks
‍Water seeping around seals and penetrations.

Tree limb impacts
Broken shingles or punctures from falling branches.
