Common roofing emergencies we handle

From storm damage to sudden leaks, our expert team repairs all types of roofing emergencies. We quickly assess the problem and restore your roof’s protection.

Tree or branch impacts
Broken shingles or punctures from falling branches.

Storm-related issues
Hail dents, lifted shingles, and wind-blown damage.

Sudden leaks
Water intrusion from ice dams or flashing failures.

Fire or smoke-damaged roofing
Severely weakened materials requiring urgent coverage.

Detached components
Flashing, ridge caps, or other roofing elements pulled loose.
