Why roof replacement matters

Your roof is the first line of defense against Wisconsin’s extreme weather—snow, rain, hail, and heat. Over time, even the most durable roof begins to degrade.

Signs you may need a roof replacement:

  • Curling, cracked, or missing shingles – Shingles warped, damaged, or gone entirely.
  • Persistent leaks or water stains – Recurring leaks and stains inside ceilings.
  • Sagging roof deck – Roof deck visibly sagging or drooping.
  • Daylight visible in your attic – Sunlight streaming through attic roofboards.
  • Energy bills creeping higher each season – Sharp rise in heating or cooling costs.
