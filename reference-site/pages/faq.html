Customer Support  FAQs
Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing plans to make your home improvement projects more affordable. Contact us to learn more about your options.

Are your products covered by warranties?

Absolutely. All our services and products come with comprehensive warranties for your peace of mind.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We provide free consultations and assessments for all our services to help you make an informed decision.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve homeowners across Wisconsin, including Milwaukee, Madison, Green Bay, Kenosha, and surrounding areas.

Can gutter protection systems handle heavy rainfall?

Yes! Our systems are engineered to withstand Wisconsin’s toughest weather conditions, including heavy rainfall and snowmelt.

How long does gutter protection installation take?

Most installations can be completed in a single day, depending on the size and complexity of your home.

Will gutter protection eliminate the need for gutter cleaning?

While it significantly reduces the frequency of cleaning, occasional maintenance may still be required to ensure optimal performance.

What is a gutter protection system?

A gutter protection system is designed to prevent debris, leaves, and pests from clogging your gutters, ensuring water flows freely and protecting your home from water damage.

How long does window installation take?

Installation typically takes one to two days, depending on the number of windows and their size.

Are your windows energy-efficient?

Yes, our windows are designed to improve insulation, reduce energy costs, and enhance comfort year-round.

What types of windows do you offer?

We offer a wide range of styles, including double-hung, casement, sliding, bay, bow, and custom windows, all with energy-efficient options.

How do I know if my windows need replacing?

Signs you may need new windows include drafts, condensation between panes, difficulty opening or closing, and high energy bills.

Do you handle storm damage repairs?

Yes, we specialize in storm damage repairs and can assist with insurance claims if needed.

How long does roof installation or replacement take?

A full roof replacement typically takes one to three days, depending on the size and complexity of the project.

What roofing materials do you offer?

We offer asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile roofing, and flat roofing options to suit various styles and budgets.

How do I know if my roof needs repair or replacement?

Look for signs like missing or damaged shingles, leaks, sagging, or water stains inside your home. If you’re unsure, schedule a free roof inspection with us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and offer financing options for eligible projects.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by filling out our [Contact Form →] or calling us directly.
